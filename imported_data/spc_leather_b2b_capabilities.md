**01 — WHO WE ARE**

Santosh Pargal & Co — trading as SPC Leather — is a fully integrated leather manufacturer based in Murthal, Sonipat, Haryana, India. Founded in 1989, we operate across the complete leather value chain: from raw hide to finished goods, entirely in-house.

We are not a trading house. We are not a buying agent. We are a manufacturer with our own tannery and our own production floor under one roof — which means we control quality, lead times, and traceability at every stage.

**05 — SUSTAINABILITY ****&**** CARBON PERFORMANCE**

As part of a structured decarbonisation initiative, we transitioned our core energy systems — including our 250 kVA power generator and hot air units — from diesel to Piped Natural Gas (PNG). Performance is calculated using verified fuel consumption data, weighted calorific values, and internationally recognised emission factors.

| **34%** **Scope-1 CO₂ reduction** Direct emissions, verified | **5–6%** **Energy carbon intensity** Improvement in kg CO₂/MJ | **Monthly** **Monitoring frequency** Continuous data-driven review |
| --- | --- | --- |

For global brands with ESG reporting requirements, Scope 3 emissions tracking, or sustainability sourcing mandates, we provide verified carbon data at the material level — lower embedded carbon per square metre of leather, without compromising quality.

Our sustainability performance is measured, not stated. Data is available to buyers on request.
